# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics book. This comprehensive guide will take you through the fundamentals of embodied intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

- ROS 2 fundamentals for robotics development
- Simulation environments with Gazebo and Unity
- NVIDIA Isaac ecosystem for robotics applications
- Voice-Language-Action (VLA) frameworks for interactive robotics
- Hardware architectures for physical AI implementations

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming knowledge
- Familiarity with command line interfaces
- Understanding of fundamental robotics concepts (helpful but not required)

Let's begin this journey into the fascinating world of physical AI and humanoid robotics!
